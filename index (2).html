These three items compound each other. Rising spend into declining lead quality inflates the cost of every downstream hour. Overloaded owners slow follow-up. Slow follow-up burns the very leads the increased spend purchased.

Left unscored, this pattern typically costs an operator in this band $18K to $27K per month before it surfaces in the P&L, because each piece looks tolerable in isolation.
